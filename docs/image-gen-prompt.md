# Waterphone Image-Gen Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority. Do not infer dimensions,
rod lengths, fill volumes, or tuning from these prompts.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a finished waterphone-style idiophone on a clean neutral studio background: a polished stainless or warm bronze resonator bowl with a central neck and handle rising from the top, a dense ring of vertical metal rods around the rim, a subtle serviceable water chamber seam, and a restrained handmade finish that shows real metal reflections and slight shop-built irregularity. Show the instrument as a real physical object ready for bowing or striking, with the rods clearly attached to the bowl rim, the neck comfortable to grip, and the bowl broad enough to suggest internal water movement. Soft directional studio lighting, realistic scale cues from a bow and small mallet laid nearby, 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop-concert scene of the waterphone being played: a musician holds the central neck while tilting the metal bowl slightly, bowing one of the rim rods with a cello bow as light catches the ring of rods and the curved bowl. The setting is a quiet maker studio or small experimental-music room with a workbench, tuner, towel, and water-fill tools in the background, natural window light, believable hand posture, and a sense of shimmering pitch-bend motion without fantasy effects.

## Detail / Macro Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the waterphone's signature mechanism: bowed metal rods fixed around the rim of a stainless or bronze resonator bowl, with the central neck and fill path softly out of focus behind them. Emphasize clean rod attachments, tiny reflections, bow hair touching one rod, subtle water-safe seams, and the contrast between brushed metal and polished contact points. High-detail macro product photography, shallow depth of field, natural metal texture.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, impossible rod paths, floating parts, extra limbs, distorted hands, fake dimensions, cutaway labels, or fantasy glow.
